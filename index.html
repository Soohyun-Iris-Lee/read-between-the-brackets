About us

Read Between the Brackets challenges how people see, share,
and understand media. We stand with Gen Z, a generation
shaped by nonstop exposure that makes them both highly fluent
and uniquely vulnerable to mis- and disinformation.

We encourage them to slow their scroll, notice how media
is constructed, and recognize that every image, caption, and clip
carries intention. Media literacy is not optional anymore.
It is a core skill for navigating the world.

Our mission is to raise critical awareness in a media-saturated
environment and help Gen Z become not just consumers of
information, but critical makers of meaning.
