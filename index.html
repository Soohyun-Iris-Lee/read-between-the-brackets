About us

Read Between the Brackets aims to make media literacy
an everyday practice. We support Gen Z, a generation
shaped by nonstop exposure that leaves them both highly
fluent and uniquely vulnerable to mis- and disinformation.



Our mission is to build critical awareness in a media-
saturated environment and help Gen Z become not just
passive consumers of information, but intentional
interpreters Media literacy is no longer optional. It is a
basic skill for navigating the world.

Our Impact

We recently launched a subway takeover across station
banners, platform billboards, and the exterior and
interior of subway cars. In a space where people absorb media continuously, this intervention creates
a moment to pause and question the content they encounter every day.



Each installation includes a QR code that links directly to our website for deeper engagement.
